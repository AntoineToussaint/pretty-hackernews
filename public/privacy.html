Hatch — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Hatch is a browser extension that renders a calmer reader for news.ycombinator.com. It is local-first and built to collect nothing.

What we collect

Nothing. Hatch has no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing activity.

Where your data lives

Your preferences — theme, read/seen stories, bookmarks, muted domains, and default feed — are stored only in your browser via chrome.storage.local. They never leave your device.

Network access

  • Hatch talks to news.ycombinator.com to display the feed and comments and, when you choose to, to vote or comment using your own existing HN login. Your HN credentials are never seen or stored by Hatch.
  • If you enable Article previews, Hatch fetches the single linked page you click “Preview” on, in order to show its text inline. Nothing is fetched unless you click Preview, and the result is not stored or sent anywhere.

Permissions

  • news.ycombinator.com access — to render the reader and act on your behalf when you vote/comment.
  • storage — to save your preferences locally.
  • declarativeNetRequest — to relax HN's content-security-policy on HN's own pages so site icons, fonts, and preview images can load. No requests are blocked or redirected.
  • Broad site access (optional) — requested only if you turn on Article previews; used solely to fetch a page you click Preview on.
